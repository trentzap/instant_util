How Instant.Utilities works
1
Admin subscribes & sets up
Purchase a plan, create your admin account, set up your facility and generate QR codes for every entry and exit.
2
Deploy QR codes
Print and mount QR codes at all building entry and exit points.
3
Managers sign up
Managers scan a QR code, create an account, and apply for manager role. Admin approves.
4
Residents sign up
Residents scan any building QR code to create their account. Manager approves their residency.
5
Everything runs itself
Residents access services, send requests, and communicate through their personalised facility portal.
